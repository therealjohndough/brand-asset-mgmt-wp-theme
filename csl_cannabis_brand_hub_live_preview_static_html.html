FAQs

Is this a software product or a service?

It’s a done-for-you service by Case Study Labs. We set up and run your brand asset hub using best-in-class tools (Corebook, Notion, WordPress, Bynder, or your stack), plus governance, workflows, and training.

Do you handle cannabis-specific compliance like age gates and disclaimers?

Yes. We implement age verification and market-specific disclaimers (e.g., CA, NY, MA) and keep a rules library so updates roll out fast.

Can you manage COAs and packaging?

We centralize COAs, map them to SKUs, and maintain packaging/label templates with required statements, nutrition/THC panels, and claim guardrails.

What about rapid product drops?

We build SKU drop workflows from lab results to retailer kits. Approvals, asset generation, and distribution happen in one trackable path.

Budgets are tight because of the green tax. Can you right-size this?

Absolutely. We’re platform-agnostic and design to your reality: start lean, automate where it matters, and scale when the market allows.
